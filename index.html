Mission Statement

H.O.P.E. Fund Inc. Mental Health & Family Services seeks to educate and collaborate in finding pathways to healing for those who are striving to improve their lives as well as those of their families.

Services Offered

We are certified to offer the following
services:

Therapeutic services 

Providing individual and group counseling, as well as education on mental health illnesses.

Case Management

Assisting with the coordination of treatment and providing ongoing support and symptom management.

Recreation and Outreach

Promoting health fitness as well as education on various sport and recreational activities. Connecting families and communities to enhance togetherness.

Why Are Our Services Important?

“We want to help those persons struggling with mental illness become the best versions of themselves and succeed during these trying times.”

In 2019, 20.6% adults experienced a mental illness (that is 1 in 5 people). In 2016, 16.5% of children ages 6-17 also experienced a mental health disorder.

Of those with a mental health disorder only 44.8% of adults and 50.6% of youth ages 6 -17 sought treatment.

Those who do not seek treatment are more likely to suffer with cardiovascular disease, drop out of high school, or end their own lives.
